Lights Out In Wonderland

Pyschology vs Philosophy

When twenty-something Gabriel Brockwell's anti-capitalist activism accidentally starts turning a profit, it's the last straw. His philosophical enquiries about the state of the world - from credit default swaps to soulless shopping malls - all point in one direction. But before he checks out of life, Gabriel decides to go on one last journey, which will take him from London to Japan and Berlin - and finally to Wonderland.
